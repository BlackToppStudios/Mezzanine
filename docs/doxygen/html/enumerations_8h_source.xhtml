57  /// @enum AttenuationStyle
58  /// @brief These values represent the kind of attenuation applied to the field strength
59  /// over a distance.
60  /// @details None is the default, where the force is constant in all area's of the
61  /// field. Linear is where the force applied drops by the attenuation value times the distance
62  /// (strength - (attenuation amount * distance to AE center)). Quadratic is where the force
63  /// applied drops by the attenuation value times the distance squared (strength -
64  /// (attenuation amount * distance to AE center * distance to AE center)).
65  enum AttenuationStyle
66  {
67  Att_None, ///< No Attentuation, Equal strength through.
68  Att_Linear, ///< Linear attentuation, Strength weaker farther from center.
69  Att_Quadratic ///< Quadratic/Exponential Attentuation, similar to real gravity, it tapers of more the further from the center you get.
70  };
